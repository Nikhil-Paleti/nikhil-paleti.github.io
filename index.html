    As an AI Engineer at TechProfuse, I developed and deployed AI models to enhance operational efficiency and data processing capabilities.

    • Led a team to label data and develop object detection models, which detect tables, columns, and rows in documents. Integrated these models into an OCR pipeline, forming the backend for a mobile application.
